TaxesRoughly Support

Please email gdombrow@mac.com regarding any issues or feature requests for TaxesRoughly.
The MacOS app has been updated for 2022 and 2023. Support for 2024 is in progress and the
application will be updated again for 2024 when the United States Internal Revenue Service (IRS)
releases the final 2024 tax bracket numbers. The iOS iPad app update is in progress.


October 16, 2023

The MacOS application has been updated for Tax Year 2023. There have not been any significant
changes as far as what is handled by this program for 2023 other than indexing the tax brackets, tax
tables, increased deductions for inflation, and increased Covid-19 related child tax credits. The
child tax credits were changed for both 2021 and 2022.


October 16, 2023

The iPad application has been updated for Tax Year 2023, additions as per the MacOS release above.
