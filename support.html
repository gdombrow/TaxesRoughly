The MacOS app has been updated for 2022 and tax year 2023 will be added when sufficient data
are made available by the United States Internal Revenue Service (IRS).

The iOS iPad app update is in progress.


February 17, 2022

The MacOS application has been updated for Tax Year 2022. There have not been any significant
changes as far as what is handled by this program for 2022 other than indexing the tax brackets, tax
tables, increased deductions for inflation, and increased Covid-19 related child tax credits. The
child tax credits were changed for both 2021 and 2022.


March 22, 2022

The iPad application has been updated for Tax Year 2022, additions as per the MacOS release above.
